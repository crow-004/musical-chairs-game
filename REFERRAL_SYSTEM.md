# Musical Chairs: On-Chain Referral System

To bootstrap community growth and reward our players, we have implemented a fully on-chain referral system that turns our players into partners.

## How It Works

The process is designed to be simple for players and secure for the protocol.

1.  **Share Your Link:** Every player has a unique referral link available on their dashboard. This link contains your wallet address as a reference. You can also use the provided QR code for easy sharing.

2.  **First-Time Join:** When a new player (a "referee") joins the game for the first time using your link, the smart contract creates a pending, on-chain referral record. This links their address to yours.

3.  **Confirm Your Referees:** To prevent abuse and confirm the relationship, you (the "referrer") must approve each new player once. This is done from the "My Referrals" dashboard with a simple signature request (no gas fee required).

4.  **Start Earning:** Once a referee is confirmed, you will earn a commission from the platform's fees for **every game they play, forever.**

## Earning Commission

Our model ensures that referrers are rewarded directly from the value generated by the platform.

*   **Commission Pool:** A portion of the platform's commission from every game is allocated to a dedicated Referral Pool within the smart contract.

*   **Proportional Distribution:** This pool is then distributed among the referrers of the players who participated in that specific game.

*   **Example:**
    *   A game with 5 players generates a platform fee.
    *   A part of this fee goes to the Referral Pool.
    *   If your referee is the **only** referred player in that game, you receive **100%** of the Referral Pool for that game.
    *   If two of your referees are in the game, and they are the only referred players, you still receive 100% of the pool.
    *   If your referee and another player's referee are in the game, the pool is split between you and the other referrer.

This system creates a powerful, decentralized, and organic growth engine where our most active community members are directly rewarded for their contribution to the ecosystem's expansion.
